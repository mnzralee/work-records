# GX Coin Hero Video Prompts for Google Veo

## Overview
Create 8-scene hero video prompts for the GX Coin marketing website homepage. Videos generated using Google Veo with 8-second scene segments. **Documentary/realistic style throughout**, telling a full narrative arc from problem → solution → vision. **No explicit GX branding** in videos—website will handle text overlays.

## Visual Guidelines (from existing prompt)
- **Lighting**: Warm, natural sunlight (golden hour style), soft, dignified, hopeful
- **Depth of Field**: Very shallow—subjects stand out beautifully
- **Focus**: Extreme focus on hands (tools of work) and focused, dignified expressions
- **Pacing**: Smooth, slow-motion, graceful
- **Emotion**: Respect for human effort, skill, quiet confidence. Real, authentic, human-centric
- **Composition**: Subjects slightly off-center to allow for text overlay

### Scene 1: "The Silent Erosion" (0-8s)
**Concept**: Hardworking people seeing the value of their labor slip away
**Narrative**: *The promise was broken—work hard, but your savings still shrink*

**DESKTOP (16:9) Prompt**:
```
Cinematic, 8-second, slow-motion, shallow depth of field. Golden hour morning light, slightly muted. A montage of three brief cuts showing dignified workers at the END of their workday—tired but proud. Cut 1 (0-3s): A baker, apron dusted with flour, counts paper bills at his empty shop counter. His expression shifts subtly from satisfaction to quiet concern as he looks at the bills. Cut 2 (3-6s): An elderly seamstress in her small workshop holds a pay envelope, her weathered hands trembling slightly as she counts. Warm light through dusty window. Cut 3 (6-8s): A teacher sits alone at her desk after class, looking at bills and receipts spread before her. Her expression is dignified but worried. All shots: subjects slightly off-center for text overlay. Photorealistic, emotional depth, film grain.
```

**MOBILE (9:16) Prompt**:
```
Vertical cinematic, 8-second, slow-motion, shallow depth of field. Golden hour light, slightly desaturated. A single continuous shot: A middle-aged grocery store owner stands behind her register at closing time. Warm dusty light streams through the window. Her hands—strong, capable hands—slowly count paper bills. Close-up intercuts between her focused face and her counting hands. Her expression transitions from routine to quiet concern as she finishes counting. The total is less than expected. She pauses, looks out the window. Dignified, not defeated. Subject slightly off-center. Photorealistic, emotional, authentic.
```
